Frequently Asked Questions About Sri Raghavendra Mess

What are your operating hours in Villupuram?

Yes, we specialize in bulk orders for festivals and events. Minimum order is 20 plates. We offer Pongal, Poori, Lemon Rice, Puli Sadam, and more.
